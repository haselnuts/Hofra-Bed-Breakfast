Dellendistrict

Dellendistrict with its 2 lakes, the South and Northern Dellen, were formed by a meteor, around 1.2km in diameter, about 90 million years ago. The meteor formed a crater, around 20km in diameter and 250m deep. An inferno with smoke, fire and stones covered the sky and destroyed all life…

Nowadays there is a lot of life here. Dellendistrict offers something for everybody. You can find a culture, nature and nice people.
During summer there is a lot of to do but most proud are of our folklore fests. You will meet people from around the world from different culture background, music and original food from the district.
Nature is always close by, hiking in the forests, fishing or just take a bath in the lakes nearby. Even during winter when the snow is here there are a lot of possibilities to do outside.

We are sure you will find something to do and book another visit right away.
We are happy to help you.

Hiking

Hiking is a must. The forests around here are filled with trails, sometimes it's even difficult to choose one.
To stop for a while and enjoy the view or the silence in the forest or just take a bath in one of the beautiful clear ponds is life.

Winter time

When the snow is here the inhabitants of the Dellendistrict awake again. Their clean up their cross country or downhill skies, kicks, fishing equipment or the snowmobile and get out on the lakes or into the forest.

Nothing is far from Hofra. A cross country ski trail is just around the corner or a bit farther in the forest. If you want to go downhill then Järvsö is not far, just about 45 minutes with the car.

Go by snowmobile, the closet trail is starting in Delsbo, we are happy to show you the way.
