The rooms!

The charming rooms are decorated in a folksy style where each room has it's own personality. The rooms are light and decorated with harmonic natural colors and material with history close by. Choose between The Suite, Red or White room.

The rooms are distributed on two levels with a common entrance from our beautiful courtyard, a wonderfully charming garden. The Suite and the Red rum are on the second floor. The White room is on the first floor as well as the charming family room, the countrystyled kitchen and the bathroom. The bathrum is shared between all three rooms.

The breakfast will be served thru personal breakfast baskets for each room in the kitchen. Enjoy your breakfast when it's suits you in the countrystyled kitchen or in the beautiful garden. The breakfast consists of organic or local food as much as possible.

Please be aware that Hofra is a non-smoking area, according to the tobacco law as valid from 1st July 2019, for in- and outdoors.

 

The Suite

A very spacious room in a romatic style with generous light from all sides. The room is decorated in a wonderful green color. This room is for the bride and groom or for the family to spend time together.

 

Red room

A light and nice room with beautiful original paperwall in light red tones. View over the garden and far mountains. The room has two single beds.

 

White room

Romantic decorated room with light colors with view over the garden and the far mountains. The room has a queen size bed. For guests with dogs this is the perfect room.
